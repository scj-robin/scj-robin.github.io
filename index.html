Stéphane Robin


I am a professor at Sorbonne Université, in the Laboratoire de Probabilités, Statistique et Modélisation (LPSM)

I am specialized in statistics, especially latent variable models, network models and change-point detection, and their applications in life sciences, formerly in genomics and genetics and now mostly in ecology
